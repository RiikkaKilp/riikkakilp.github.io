Art Lead

Sabreurs - A Noble Duel (09/2018)

Producer

Panic! At The Kitchen (09/2018)

Producer/Designer

Panic! At The Kitchen was a game made in two weeks for theInternational Game Concept Challengewith international teams set by the organizers. The game is a 4-player local multiplayer with heavy emphasis on player communication. Our team had a total of six people. Due to us having only one programmer, I also did some programming like the level randomization, shaders and worked with Unitys NavMesh. VFX, implementing the assets and making colliders was my responsibility. Our team won the challenge, due to the most market potential and refreshing take on the communication between players.
